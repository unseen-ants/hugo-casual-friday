Hugo is one of the fastest static site generators available. This guide will help you get started with your first Hugo site.

## Why Hugo?

Hugo offers several advantages:

- **Speed**: Hugo builds sites in milliseconds
- **Flexibility**: Powerful templating system
- **No dependencies**: Single binary, no runtime required
